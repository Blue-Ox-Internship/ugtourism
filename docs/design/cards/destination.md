# Destination Cards

## Role in the Game
Destination cards are the most valuable cards in the game. They represent places in Uganda that players travel to, and they are also a player's **lives**. Lose all your Destination cards and you are eliminated. Each round is started by a player placing one of their Destination cards in the centre — it sets the region all other players must match.

## Count
**32 cards** in the deck. Each player starts with 3.

## Front Design

### Layout
- **Top left** — Region badge: colored circle with bold letter (C / W / N / E)
- **Top right** — Small label: "Destination" in uppercase tracking
- **Center** — Full bleed painterly illustration of the destination
- **Bottom** — Dark gradient overlay with destination name (bold serif) and region name below (small sans-serif)
- **Bottom right corner** — Small QR code in a decorative rounded frame, labeled "Scan to Unlock" (Destination cards are the primary QR-enabled cards)

### Illustration Direction
Dramatic, landscape-focused. Show the destination at its most spectacular — golden hour light, scale, atmosphere. The viewer should feel the urge to visit. Painterly, warm, rich detail.
