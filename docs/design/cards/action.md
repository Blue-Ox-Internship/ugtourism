# Action Cards

## Role in the Game
Action cards are the wildcard chaos of the game. They represent funny, unexpected, and memorable travel situations that happen to real travellers in Uganda. Played immediately when drawn or chosen from hand. They belong to **no region** and create instant effects without earning or losing replacement draws.

Action cards are designed to make people laugh and create stories. The best game sessions are remembered by the Action cards — not the scores.

## Count
**18 cards** in the deck. None dealt at the start — drawn from the Main Deck during play.

---

## Front Design

### Layout
- **Top left** — No region badge (Action cards are regionless) — replaced by a **⚡ lightning bolt icon** or **★ star** in gold
- **Top right** — Small label: "Action" in uppercase tracking, in a bright accent color
- **Center** — Full bleed painterly illustration of the funny situation
- **Bottom** — Dark gradient overlay with the action name (bold serif) and the effect in clear italic text below

### Illustration Direction
Expressive, humorous, slightly exaggerated. Cartoon-adjacent but still quality illustration. Show the funny moment — the flat tyre on a remote road, the monkey stealing food, the rainstorm catching a traveller off-guard. Characters should have personality and expression. Warm tones, but more dynamic and energetic than other card types.

### Special Visual Detail
A **bright gold or amber glow** on the border of Action card fronts signals to players: something is about to happen. The lightning bolt in place of a region badge is immediately recognisable as "not a normal card."
