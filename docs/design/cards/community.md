# Community Cards

## Role in the Game
Community cards represent Uganda's people, tribes, and traditions. They are played during a Journey to match the region of the active Destination card. Before playing, the player reads the card's fact aloud — teaching everyone at the table a greeting, a cultural tradition, or something about that community.

## Count
**24 cards** in the deck. Each player starts with 2.

## Front Design

### Layout
- **Top left** — Region badge: colored circle with bold letter (C / W / N / E)
- **Top right** — Small label: "Community" in uppercase tracking
- **Center** — Full bleed painterly illustration of people, community life, or cultural practice
- **Bottom** — Dark gradient overlay with tribe/community name (bold serif) and region below
- **Bottom section** — "Did You Know?" fact panel visible in gradient area or on a small strip

### Illustration Direction
Warm, human, celebratory. Show people in traditional dress, cultural ceremonies, community life. Respectful, dignified, vibrant. Not stereotypical — show real cultural richness. Golden tones, fabric textures, community warmth.
