🌍 TRAVEL CARDS (Universal)

Rule: A Travel Card may be played for any region.

1. Local Tour Guide
🧭 Every Journey Is Better with a Guide

A local guide knows the hidden stories, best routes, and unforgettable places that maps often miss.

2. Camera
📸 Capture the Moment

Every destination has a story worth remembering. A photograph lets you take a piece of Uganda home with you.

3. Backpack
🎒 Ready for Anything

A well-packed backpack means you're prepared for every adventure, whether it's a city walk or a mountain hike.

4. Uganda Travel Map
🗺️ Choose Your Next Adventure

From waterfalls and forests to lakes and mountains, Uganda is full of places waiting to be explored.

5. Reusable Water Bottle
💧 Stay Refreshed

Exploring is more enjoyable when you stay hydrated, especially during long journeys.

6. Binoculars
🔭 See More

Keep your eyes open—Uganda's incredible birds and wildlife are often hiding in plain sight.

7. Hiking Boots
🥾 Adventure Starts with the First Step

Comfortable boots make every trail easier, from the slopes of Mount Elgon to the forests of Bwindi.

8. Travel Journal
📖 Every Journey Has a Story

Write down the people you meet, the foods you taste, and the places you'll never forget.

9. Souvenir
🎁 Take Home a Memory

A handmade souvenir carries the story of the place you visited and the people who made it.

10. Friendship
🤝 The Best Souvenir Isn't Something You Buy

The friendships you make while travelling often become the memories you treasure the most.
