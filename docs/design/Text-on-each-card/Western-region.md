🗺️ DESTINATION CARDS (Western)
1. Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

Region: Western

Meet the Gentle Giants

Deep within Bwindi's ancient rainforest live almost half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas. A trek through this forest is one of Africa's greatest wildlife adventures.

2. Queen Elizabeth National Park

Region: Western

Where Lions Climb Trees

Queen Elizabeth National Park is one of the few places on Earth where lions are often seen relaxing in the branches of fig trees.

3. Lake Bunyonyi

Region: Western

The Lake of Many Little Birds

Lake Bunyonyi is dotted with nearly 30 islands and is one of Uganda's most peaceful and scenic destinations.

4. Lake Mburo National Park

Region: Western

Home of the Zebra

Lake Mburo National Park is Uganda's best place to spot zebras, impalas, and elegant elands roaming freely across the savannah.

5. Rwenzori Mountains

Region: Western

Mountains of the Moon

The Rwenzori Mountains are Africa's highest mountain range after Kilimanjaro and are famous for their snow-capped peaks near the Equator.

6. Kibale National Park

Region: Western

Chimpanzee Capital

Kibale Forest is one of the best places in the world to see wild chimpanzees in their natural habitat.

7. Sempaya Hot Springs

Region: Western

Nature's Boiling Wonder

At Sempaya Hot Springs, water bubbles from deep underground hot enough to boil eggs in just a few minutes.

8. Crater Lakes

Region: Western

A Landscape Painted by Volcanoes

Western Uganda is home to dozens of beautiful crater lakes formed by ancient volcanic activity, creating breathtaking scenery.
👥 COMMUNITY CARDS – WESTERN REGION

These shouldn't just name tribes. They should introduce players to the people who make Western Uganda unique.

1. Banyankole
🐄 Keepers of the Long-Horned Cattle

The Banyankole are famous for raising the beautiful Ankole long-horned cattle, a symbol of wealth, pride, and tradition in Western Uganda.

2. Bakiga
⛰️ People of the Hills

The Bakiga are known for their hard work and resilience, farming the steep hillsides of southwestern Uganda for generations.

3. Batooro
👑 The Kingdom of Tooro

The Batooro are the people of the Tooro Kingdom, where culture, royal traditions, and colourful ceremonies continue to thrive today.

4. Banyoro
🏛️ The Legacy of Bunyoro

The Banyoro belong to one of Uganda's oldest kingdoms, once home to powerful kings who ruled vast parts of the region.

5. Runyankole Greeting
👋 A Warm Welcome

When greeting someone in Runyankole, you can say "Agandi?" meaning "How are you?" A friendly greeting is the first step to good conversation.

6. Ekitaguriro Dance
💃 Dance with Energy

Ekitaguriro is a lively traditional dance of the Banyankole, celebrated with energetic movements and joyful singing during special occasions.

7. Ankole Long-Horned Cattle
🐂 More Than Just Cattle

With their impressive horns, Ankole cattle are admired not only for their beauty but also for their important place in Western Uganda's culture and history.

🎒 EXPERIENCE CARDS – WESTERN REGION

These cards should make players think,

"I want to try that!"

1. Gorilla Trekking
🦍 A Once-in-a-Lifetime Encounter

Meeting mountain gorillas in the wild is one of Uganda's most unforgettable adventures. Keep your distance, stay quiet, and enjoy the moment.

2. Chimpanzee Tracking
🐵 Follow the Forest Calls

Listen carefully as expert guides lead you through the forest to find playful chimpanzees swinging through the trees.

3. Tea Plantation Tour
🍃 Where Every Cup Begins

Walk through endless green tea fields and discover how fresh tea leaves become the drink enjoyed around the world.

4. Boat Safari
🚤 Wildlife from the Water

Cruise along the Kazinga Channel and watch elephants, buffaloes, hippos, crocodiles, and birds gather along the shoreline.

5. Mountain Hiking
🥾 One Step at a Time

The trails of the Rwenzori Mountains reward hikers with breathtaking views, cool mountain air, and unforgettable scenery.

6. Crater Lake Visit
🌋 Nature's Hidden Gems

The peaceful crater lakes of Western Uganda offer perfect spots for photography, birdwatching, and quiet reflection.

7. Cultural Village Visit
🛖 Live Like a Local

Spend time with local communities, learn traditional crafts, listen to stories, and discover everyday life beyond the cities.

🍽️ TASTE CARDS – WESTERN REGION

Food should make players hungry!

1. Eshabwe
🥛 The Cream of Ankole

Made from ghee and carefully prepared ingredients, Eshabwe is one of the most treasured traditional dishes of the Banyankole.

2. Millet Bread
🌾 A Timeless Favourite

Millet bread has been enjoyed for generations and is often served alongside traditional sauces during family meals.

3. Fresh Cow's Milk
🥛 Straight from the Farm

In cattle-keeping communities, fresh milk is more than a drink—it's a symbol of hospitality and everyday life.

4. Ghee
🧈 Liquid Gold

Prepared from butter through a traditional process, ghee adds its rich flavour to many Western Ugandan dishes.

5. Matooke
🍌 A Ugandan Classic

Steamed green bananas, known as matooke, are a staple meal enjoyed across Uganda and especially loved in the west.

6. Roasted Goat
🔥 A Celebration Favourite

Roasted goat meat is often shared during celebrations, bringing friends and families together around the fire.

7. Groundnut Sauce
🥜 Simple but Delicious

Creamy groundnut sauce pairs perfectly with matooke, millet bread, cassava, and many other traditional meals.
