⚡ ACTION CARDS

These cards should be the funniest part of the game.

I think they should make everyone at the table laugh.

The situations should feel like things that could actually happen while travelling around Uganda.

1. Wrong Greeting 😅
Oops!

You greeted an elder using the wrong words.

Action

Pass one Destination Card to the player on your right.

2. Flat Tyre 🛞
Not Again!

Your vehicle has broken down.

Action

Miss this journey.

3. Lost Luggage 🧳
Where Did It Go?

Your luggage has taken its own adventure.

Action

Discard one random card from your hand.

4. Monkey Business 🐒
Hey! That's My Food!

A cheeky monkey has stolen your lunch.

Action

Draw one card from the Main Deck.

5. Heavy Rain 🌧️
Roads Are Flooded

Your journey is delayed by bad weather.

Action

Skip your next turn.

6. Festival Invitation 🎉
Everyone Is Welcome!

You've been invited to join a local celebration.

Action

Swap one card in your hand with any player.

7. Boda Boda Shortcut 🏍️
You Got There Faster!

The boda rider knew a quicker route.

Action

Draw two cards from the Main Deck, then discard one.

8. Friendly Local 😊
Need Some Help?

A local offers helpful directions.

Action

Take one random card from another player's hand.

9. Missed the Bus 🚌
Better Luck Next Time

You arrived just as the bus was leaving.

Action

Miss this journey.

10. Street Market
Too Many Choices!

You spent more time shopping than travelling.

Action

Discard one card, then draw one.

11. Fuel Stop ⛽
Time for a Break

Every road trip needs fuel.

Action

Nothing happens. Continue your journey.

(A "good luck" card that breaks up the chaos.)

12. Tour Group 📢
New Friends!

Travelling with others makes every trip more enjoyable.

Action

Every player draws one card from the Main Deck.

13. Road Diversion 🚧
Detour Ahead

The road is closed.

Action

Choose another player to miss this journey instead of you.

14. Wildlife Crossing 🦒
Stop and Watch

Animals have the right of way!

Action

Skip your turn, then draw one card.

15. Lost Signal 📱
No Internet Today

You'll have to ask the locals for directions.

Action

Reveal your hand to all players.

16. Free Upgrade ⭐
Lucky You!

Your accommodation has been upgraded.

Action

Draw one Destination Card.

17. Passport Check 🛂
Documents, Please

Officials need to verify your travel documents.

Action

Show your Destination Cards. Nothing else happens.

(This is a lighthearted, low-impact card.)

18. Travel Like a Ugandan ❤️
You've Embraced the Journey

You greeted people warmly, respected local culture, and enjoyed every experience.

Action

Protect yourself from the next Action Card played against you.
