# Region Design System

## Overview

Every non-Action card belongs to one of Uganda's four regions. The region is the core mechanic of the game — players must match the region of the active Destination card to stay in the journey.

The region system must be instantly readable, even for children who cannot read yet. A single bold letter and a strong color does the job.

---

## The Four Regions + Universal

| Region | Initial | Color Name | Hex | RGB | Used On |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Central | **C** | Forest Green | `#2D6A4F` | 45, 106, 79 | All Central cards |
| Western | **W** | Deep Blue | `#1B4F8A` | 27, 79, 138 | All Western cards |
| Northern | **N** | Gold / Amber | `#E5A020` | 229, 160, 32 | All Northern cards |
| Eastern | **E** | Deep Red | `#C0392B` | 192, 57, 43 | All Eastern cards |
| Universal | **U** | Silver / Slate | `#8E9BAE` | 142, 155, 174 | All Journey cards |

---

## Region Badge Design (Front of Card — Top Left)

```
  ┌───┐
  │ C │   ← bold letter, white
  └───┘   ← filled circle in region color
```

- Shape: filled circle, approximately 28px diameter on card
- Letter: white, bold, large — readable at a glance
- Position: top-left corner of card front, small padding from edge
- No text label needed — the letter alone is enough once players know the system

## What Each Region Covers

### 🟢 Central (C)
**Color: Forest Green**

The political, commercial, and cultural heart of Uganda. Home to the capital Kampala, Lake Victoria, and the Buganda Kingdom — the most powerful traditional kingdom in Uganda's history.

Key destinations: Kampala, Kasubi Tombs, Lake Victoria, Ssese Islands, Mabira Forest, Entebbe, Mpanga Forest, Bujagali Falls

Key communities: Baganda, Basoga, Banyara

---

### 🔵 Western (W)
**Color: Deep Blue**

Uganda's most biodiverse region. Home to mountain gorillas, chimpanzees, the Rwenzori Mountains, and the Ankole cattle culture. The most visited region by international tourists.

Key destinations: Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Rwenzori Mountains, Queen Elizabeth NP, Lake Bunyonyi, Kibale Forest, Fort Portal, Mgahinga, Semuliki

Key communities: Banyankole, Bakiga, Batooro, Banyoro

---

### 🟡 Northern (N)
**Color: Gold / Amber**

Uganda's most resilient region. Home to the Acholi and Langi people, the mighty Murchison Falls, and the Albert Nile. A region of strong culture, open savannah, and dramatic river landscapes.

Key destinations: Murchison Falls, Gulu, Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary, Budongo Forest, Karuma Falls, Pakwach, Pian Upe Reserve

Key communities: Acholi, Langi, Alur, Madi

---

### 🔴 Eastern (E)
**Color: Deep Red**

Uganda's adventure region. Home to the Source of the Nile, Sipi Falls, Mount Elgon, and Kidepo Valley — the most dramatic and remote park in all of Uganda.

Key destinations: Jinja & Source of the Nile, Sipi Falls, Mount Elgon NP, Kidepo Valley NP, Mbale, Lake Mburo NP, Tororo, Soroti

Key communities: Iteso, Bagisu, Karamojong, Basabiny, Banyole

---

### ⬜ Universal (U)
**Color: Silver / Slate**

Journey cards belong to no region. The U badge is neutral and silver — distinguishing it visually from all four regional colors. Universal cards can enter any journey regardless of the active destination's region.

Journey cards: Tour Guide, Backpack, Camera, Travel Map, Bus Ticket, Journal, Binoculars, Compass, Torch, Water Bottle

---

## Using the Region System in Digital Design (Website)

The same 5 colors above should be used consistently across the website wherever regions are referenced:

- Region filter buttons on the Explore page
- Destination card region labels
- Leaderboard regional categories
- Any map or Uganda visualization
- Partner listings filtered by region

This creates visual consistency between the physical cards and the digital experience.
